<h3 class="Cite" id="4-2-110G"><a name="4-2-110G"></a>4-2-110G DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS FOR COTTAGE HOUSE DEVELOPMENT:</h3>
<p class="P1"><span class="bold">1.&nbsp;Purpose:</span> The provisions of this subsection are available as alternatives to the development of typical detached single-family homes with the intention of generating housing types that are responsive to changing household demographics and homeownership opportunities in single-family neighborhoods.</p>

<p class="P1"><span class="bold">2.&nbsp;Applicability: </span>This subsection applies to proposed cottage house developments in residential zones R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, and R-14. An existing single-family home incorporated into a cottage house development that does not meet the requirements of this subsection is allowed to remain on site. Proposed modifications or additions to the structure not consistent with the provisions of this subsection shall not be permitted.</p>

<p class="P1"><span class="bold">3.&nbsp;Limitations: </span>No more than one hundred (100) cottage houses shall be permitted Citywide in a calendar year.</p>
