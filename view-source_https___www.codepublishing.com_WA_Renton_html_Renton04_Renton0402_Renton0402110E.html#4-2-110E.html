<h3 class="Cite" id="4-2-110E"><a name="4-2-110E"></a>4-2-110E DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS FOR DETACHED ACCESSORY BUILDINGS<sup><a name="wwfootnote_inline_240" href="#240">1</a></sup></h3>
<table class="Format_A" style="margin-bottom: 12.0pt; margin-left: 0pt; width: 468.62628000000006pt" cellspacing="0" summary="">
<tr>
<td style="background-color: #B2B2B2;border: 1px solid black; width: 468.62628000000006pt" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">MAXIMUM NUMBER AND SIZE</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top; width: 166.00908pt">
<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top; width: 302.6172pt">
<p class="Cellright" >Accessory structures shall only be allowed on lots in conjunction with a primary use.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >The total floor area of all accessory buildings shall not be greater than the floor area of the primary residential uses.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >The lot coverage of the primary residential structure combined with all accessory buildings shall not exceed the maximum lot coverage of the Zoning District.<span class="superscript">17</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RC and R-1</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >2 structures &ndash; max. 720 sq. ft. per structure, or</p>

<p class="Cellright" >1 structure &ndash; max. 1,000 sq. ft.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >In addition, 1 barn or stable &ndash; max. 2,000 sq. ft., provided the lot is 5 acres or more.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >R-4, R-6, and R-8</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >2 structures &ndash; max. 720 sq. ft. per structure, or</p>

<p class="Cellright" >1 structure &ndash; max. 1,000 sq. ft.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >R-10 and R-14</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >1 structure per residential unit &ndash; max. 400 sq. ft.; provided, that they are architecturally consistent with the principal structure.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >Except greenhouses, sheds, or other similar accessory structures &ndash; max. 150 sq. ft.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="background-color: #B2B2B2;border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">MAXIMUM HEIGHT</span><span class="superscript" >18, 19</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">General</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>

<p class="Cellright" >RC</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >12 ft.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >R-1, R-4, R-6, and R-8</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >12 ft.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >Animal husbandry or agricultural related structures are subject to the maximum wall plate height of RMC <a href="../../Renton04/Renton0402/Renton0402110.html#4-2-110">4-2-110</a>A, and associated conditions. Additionally, the structure shall not be taller than the primary dwelling.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >R-10 and R-14</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >12 ft.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >Agricultural related structures are subject to the maximum wall plate height of RMC <a href="../../Renton04/Renton0402/Renton0402110.html#4-2-110">4-2-110</a>A, and associated conditions, except that the structure shall not be taller than the primary dwelling.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RMF and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >25 ft.<span class="superscript">20</span>, except that the structure shall not be taller than the primary building(s).</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">Public Facilities</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >Maximum height for public facilities shall be determined through site plan review.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>

<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >See RMC <a href="../../Renton04/Renton0404/Renton0404140.html#4-4-140">4-4-140</a>, Wireless Communication Facilities, for maximum height requirements. Freestanding vertical monopole amateur radio antennas are allowed a maximum height of 45 ft. without a Conditional Use Permit. Taller structures will have maximum height determined pursuant to RMC <a href="../../Renton04/Renton0409/Renton0409030.html#4-9-030">4-9-030</a>, Conditional Use Permits.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">Clear Vision Area</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >In no case shall a structure over 42 in. in height intrude into the 20 ft. clear vision area defined in RMC <a href="../../Renton04/Renton0411/Renton0411030.html#4-11-030">4-11-030</a>.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="background-color: #B2B2B2;border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">LOCATION</span></p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >4 ft. from any residential structure. If sited closer than 4 ft., the structure shall be considered to be attached.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >RC, R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, and R-14</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >For any lot that abuts an alley, vehicular access to garages or carports shall be through the alley.</p>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >R-14</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >When lots do not abut an alley, all garages and carports shall be located in the rear yard or side yard.</p>

</td>
</tr>

</tr>
<tr>
<td style="border: 1px solid black;" colspan="2">
<p class="CellMain" ><span class="bold">Rear Yards for Accessory Buildings</span></p>

<p class="Cellright" >RC</p>

</td>

<p class="Cellright" >5 ft.</p>

</td>

<tr>

<p class="Cellright" >R-1, R-4, R-6, R-8, R-10, R-14, RMF, and RMF-2</p>

</td>
<td style="border: 1px solid black; vertical-align: top">
<p class="Cellright" >3 ft., unless located between the rear of the house and the rear property line, then 0 ft. rear yard is allowed.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >When located within 10 ft. of the rear property line, at least twenty-five percent (25%) of the lineal length of the rear yard shall remain unoccupied from accessory structures, except when the rear property line abuts an alley.</p>

<p class="Cellright" >Except for garages/carports accessed through alleys: to ensure adequate vehicular maneuvering area, garages and carports that are accessed through alleys shall be set back as follows:</p>

<p class="Cellright" >1. 9 ft. garage doors shall be at least 26 ft. from the back edge of the alley, or</p>

<p class="Cellright" >2. 16 ft. garage doors shall be at least 24 ft. from the back edge of the alley.</p>
